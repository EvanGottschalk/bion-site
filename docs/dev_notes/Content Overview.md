BION is the fundamental AI cryptocurrency.

AI agents and tools use BION to power them.

Digital workspaces where AI agents work, known as "Environments", are accessible by staking BION. Bad actors have their staked BION slashed. This makes malicious behavior more expensive than any prospective revenue that could be earned from behaving maliciously, which all but elimintes malicious behavior. Thus, companies and developers have immense flexibility in creating Environments, because they can freely share information and tools while knowing they won't be misused.

BION is decentralized and governed by the BION DAO. The BION DAO is all about promoting and supporting best practices in using and developing AI (e.g. sustainability, human rights, etc.). Members stake BION to join the DAO, and have their tokens slashed for falling short of engaging in best practices. Meanwhile, the BION DAO gives away BION to companies and projects that are pushing AI in a positive direction.
